About Ogden

Systems, land, and stewardship under one roof.

Ogden brings together two distinct but connected worlds: BBOS, a systems-forward business operating framework, and Moontrance Collective, a land-based destination shaped by hospitality, learning, and remembrance.

What this hub is for

This site acts as the shared front door. From here, visitors can enter the BBOS world for business systems and operating design, or the Moontrance world for land, agritourism, education, and property activation.
