⚖ Steward · Mandatory Disclaimer
This is a design assistance tool, not a professional engineering or legal determination. All scores and findings are derived from publicly available federal datasets and are subject to their accuracy limitations. Data currency varies by region — SSURGO soil surveys, NHD hydrological features, and FEMA FIRM panels are updated on independent cycles and may not reflect recent changes. No output from this tool shall be used as the sole basis for land purchase, construction, agricultural planning, or any legal decision.

Site verification is always required. Engage licensed surveyors, soil scientists, and engineers before making project-level commitments. The principle of Accessibility of Wisdom guides this tool: information should be usable by landowners and community elders alike — but never in place of professional judgment.
