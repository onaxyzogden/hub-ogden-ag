Our Purpose

We exist to restore your relationship with the Creator

Moontrance Collective is a values-rooted agritourism destination being developed around a simple belief: that time spent on the land — learning, resting, and reflecting — can restore something essential in us.

We are designing a place that brings together hospitality, education, and Islamic values in one living, breathing property. Every experience will help guests connect with the land, learn sustainable ways of living, and encounter the beauty of Allah’s creation.

From guided visits to overnight stays, each offering is being shaped by the same conviction: that beauty and values are meant to work together — not compete.

We have not yet acquired land. What you see here is the vision we are building toward — honestly and intentionally.

Stewardship
Caring for the land as a trust, not a resource to exploit. Every system on the future property will reflect this principle.
Learning
Practical education rooted in sustainable living and Islamic principles. Leave with insight you can carry home.
Remembrance
Creating space for reflection, gratitude, and connection to what matters most. Some of the best dhikr happens in between.
Hospitality
Welcoming guests with dignity, care, and a commitment to halal values. No one will ever need to check or compromise.

Our Hospitality Standard

Rooted in Islamic values,
shaped by care

We believe the best hospitality does not ask you to compromise who you are. Our destination will be designed so that Muslim families, couples, and individuals can experience something genuinely beautiful and genuinely aligned with their faith.
I

Halal, always

From food to atmosphere, every hospitality choice will be made with halal standards at the centre. Guests will never need to check or compromise.
II

Privacy-conscious design

Accommodations and shared spaces will be designed with privacy, dignity, and family comfort in mind. Safety will be built into the structure.
III

Prayer-friendly rhythms

Every experience will be paced with salah in mind. Prayer spaces, direction, and quiet time will be built naturally into the schedule.
IV

Values-safe for the family

Parents will be able to relax knowing this is an environment built around the same values they bring to their homes. No filtering needed.

A place where beauty and values are meant to work together — not compete. Every detail will be shaped so that the experience feels genuinely restful, genuinely beautiful, and genuinely aligned with faith.
