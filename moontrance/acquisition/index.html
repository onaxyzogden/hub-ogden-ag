The Problem

Most buyers waste months on the wrong land.

  • You find a property you love — but 60 days later discover the zoning doesn't allow farm hospitality.
  • You visit 20 parcels without a system. They blur together. You can't remember what made one better than another.
  • You negotiate an offer before you know if the land can physically support the six site zones your concept requires.
  • You underestimate how much usable acreage you actually need once wetlands, setbacks, and operations are carved out.
  • You lose the right property to someone who moved faster — because you weren't ready.

Recommended Workflow

Five clear stages. No guessing about what comes next.

  1. 01

    Find & Log

    Add any property from MLS, Realtor.ca, or off-market referrals to the Pipeline. Source, municipality, stage.

  2. 02

    10-Minute Reality Check

    Run the Intake Scorer on every new lead. Score populates the Pipeline automatically. A-tier only gets more attention.

  3. 03

    Deep Evaluation

    Serious candidates get the full Parcel Scorecard + Profit Traits treatment. Red flag penalties adjust your score honestly.

  4. 04

    Site Visit & Feasibility

    Log your on-site observations. Then run the Feasibility Gate before entering any negotiation — any critical "No" is a stop sign.

  5. 05

    Offer Decision

    Compare final candidates side by side. Offer only when the data says Go — and you've pressure-tested the offer economics.
