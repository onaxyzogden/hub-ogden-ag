The Offer Portfolio

Five distinct experiences,
each with its own projected role

Each offer is designed to serve a different guest, a different moment, and a different commercial function.

The Guided Tour is the front door — where strangers become guests. The Seasonal Workshop builds education credibility and repeat local traffic. The Family Land Day is the mission flagship — the experience people tell others about.

The Rest & Reconnect Stay is projected to be the portfolio’s cleanest profit engine: $141.67 per staff hour at 68.4% contribution margin. And the Family Stewardship Stay would be the strongest differentiation offer — premium, hosted, and deeply aligned with the Moontrance vision.

Portfolio Strategy

How the offers are designed to work together

01

The Conversion Engine

The Guided Tour is modeled at 100% utilisation — the front door where first-time visitors become repeat guests and future workshop attendees. Simplest to operate, most important to fill.
02

The Education Flywheel

Seasonal Workshop is projected at $67.77 net per staff hour — highest among day offers. Monthly workshops build local credibility and a repeating, loyal audience that becomes your best marketing channel.
03

The Profit Anchor

Rest & Reconnect is projected as the cleanest commercial engine — $141.67 gross per staff hour at 68.4% contribution margin. Lower touch, higher yield. Prioritise lodging quality to justify the rate.
04

The Mission Flagships

Both Family offers carry five-star Mission Alignment and Repeat Booking Potential. They generate the highest gross revenues and are the experiences guests will tell others about.
05

The Labour Reality

Family offers each require 13 labour hours per booking — the most intensive. Streamlining setup and logistics is the single biggest lever for improving already-healthy margins.
06

The Untapped Upside

Add-on revenue is at zero in the Guided Tour. A take-home product bundle could add $30–60 per booking with near-zero marginal labour cost — a meaningful lift.
