Five clear stages

No guessing about what comes next.

  1. 01

    Find & Log

    Add any property from MLS, Realtor.ca, or off-market referrals to the Pipeline. Source, municipality, stage — logged from first sighting.

  2. 02

    10-Minute Reality Check

    Run the Intake Scorer on every new lead. 10 weighted criteria, auto-assigned tier. A-tier only gets more attention.

  3. 03

    Deep Evaluation

    Serious candidates get the full Parcel Scorecard plus Profit Traits treatment. Red flag penalties adjust the score honestly.

  4. 04

    Site Visit & Feasibility

    Structured on-site observations. Then the Feasibility Gate — any critical "No" is a stop sign before negotiation.

  5. 05

    Offer Decision

    Compare final candidates side by side. Offer only when the data says Go — and the economics have been pressure-tested.
